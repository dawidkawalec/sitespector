# Pricing Strategy
## SiteSpector.app - Revenue Model

**Target Market:** Poland (MVP), Agencies/Freelancers/SMBs  
**Launch Date:** Q1 2026

---

## 💰 Pricing Tiers

### 1. Starter (99 PLN/month ~ $25 USD)
**Target:** Individual freelancers, small consultants
- 10 audits/month
- All features included
- Email support (48h response)
- 6 months report history
- Basic priority

**Positioning:** Entry-level, affordable

---

### 2. Professional (299 PLN/month ~ $75 USD)
**Target:** Active freelancers, small agencies (2-5 people)
- 50 audits/month
- All features included
- Priority email support (24h)
- 12 months report history
- White-label reports (remove branding)
- Team members: 3

**Positioning:** Sweet spot, best value

---

### 3. Agency (799 PLN/month ~ $200 USD)
**Target:** Established agencies (5-20 people)
- Unlimited audits
- All features included
- Priority support (12h response)
- Unlimited history
- Custom branding (logo, colors)
- Team members: Unlimited
- API access (100 calls/day)
- Dedicated account manager

**Positioning:** Premium, enterprise-lite

---

### 4. Pay-per-Report (29 PLN/audit ~ $7 USD)
**Target:** Occasional users, testing
- No subscription
- Single audit purchase
- All features included
- 30-day report access
- No support

**Positioning:** Try-before-buy

## 🎯 Competitive Analysis

### vs. Semrush Site Audit
- **Semrush:** $119/month (~480 PLN)
- **SiteSpector Starter:** 99 PLN (5x cheaper)
- **Advantage:** Price, AI recommendations
- **Disadvantage:** Fewer crawled pages (1 page vs 100)

### vs. Screaming Frog
- **SF:** £149/year (~740 PLN) one-time
- **SiteSpector:** 99-799 PLN/month
- **Advantage:** No setup, cloud-based, AI analysis
- **Disadvantage:** Higher monthly cost

### vs. Manual Audits (Freelancer)
- **Freelancer:** 500-2000 PLN per audit
- **SiteSpector:** 9.90-29 PLN per audit
- **Advantage:** 20-100x cheaper, instant
- **Disadvantage:** Less customized

**Positioning:** Budget-friendly automation with AI intelligence

## 🚀 Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring
- Show Agency tier first (highest price)
- Makes Professional look like "best deal"

### Decoy Effect
- Starter: $25/month (10 audits) = $2.50/audit
- **Professional: $75/month (50 audits) = $1.50/audit** ← BEST VALUE
- Agency: $200/month (unlimited) = <$0.50/audit

→ Professional appears 40% cheaper per audit

### Social Proof
- "Most Popular" badge on Professional
- "500+ agencies trust us"
- Testimonials on pricing page

## 📉 Churn Prevention

### At-Risk Indicators
- No audits in 30 days
- Only 1-2 audits/month on Starter

### Retention Tactics
- Email: "Your site score dropped!" (re-engagement)
- Discount offer: 3 months for price of 2
- Feature education: "Did you know you can...?"

**Target Churn:** <10% monthly
