# Product Roadmap
## SiteSpector.app - 18 Month Plan

---

## 🎯 ETAP 1: MVP (Months 1-2) ✅

**Goal:** Single-page technical audit with AI

- Single URL audits (no multi-page crawl)
- SEO technical analysis (Screaming Frog)
- Core Web Vitals (Lighthouse)
- AI content analysis (Claude)
- Local SEO auto-detection
- Competitor comparison (3 URLs)
- 40-page PDF report
- Dashboard + Authentication

**Launch:** End of Month 2  
**Target:** 100 paying customers

---

## 🚀 ETAP 2: UI/UX & Multi-Page (Months 3-6)

**Goal:** Screenshot analysis + crawl multiple pages

### Features
- **Screenshot capture** (Playwright)
- **Visual analysis** (AI checks: CTA placement, mobile responsiveness)
- **Multi-page crawl** (up to 100 pages per audit)
- **Historical tracking** (track score over time, monthly re-audits)
- **Scheduled audits** (auto-run weekly/monthly)
- **Email notifications** (score changes, issues found)

**Launch:** Month 6  
**Target:** 500 customers, 50k PLN MRR

---

## 🌟 ETAP 3: Advanced & International (Months 7-12)

**Goal:** Ahrefs integration + worldwide expansion

### Features
- **Ahrefs API integration**
  - Backlink analysis
  - Domain Rating (DR)
  - Keyword rankings
  - Competitor backlinks
- **Multi-language support**
  - UI: EN, DE, ES, FR
  - Reports: Translated by AI
- **International markets**
  - US, UK, Germany launch
  - Localized pricing (PPP)
- **White-label reseller program**
  - Agencies can rebrand
  - Custom domains (reports.agency.com)

**Launch:** Month 12  
**Target:** 2000 customers, 200k PLN MRR

---

## 🔮 FUTURE (Months 13-18+)
