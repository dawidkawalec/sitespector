## 🔍 Deep Dive: Semrush Site Audit

### Strengths
- 100 pages crawled per audit (vs our 1 page in MVP)
- Historical tracking (we'll add in Etap 2)
- Integrations with GA, GSC
- Brand recognition

### Weaknesses
- **No AI recommendations** (generic advice)
- **No code snippets** (just tells you what's wrong, not how to fix)
- **Expensive** ($119/mo vs our $25)
- **Complex UI** (steep learning curve)

### Our Advantage
- **AI-powered actionable advice** ("Change H1 to: X, code: `<h1>X</h1>`")
- **Simpler** (single-purpose tool)
- **Cheaper** (5x cheaper for freelancers)

---

## 🔍 Deep Dive: Screaming Frog

### Strengths
- Industry standard (everyone knows it)
- Unlimited crawling (we limit to 100 pages in Etap 2)
- Powerful filters and exports
- One-time payment (£149/year)

### Weaknesses
- **Desktop software** (not cloud, no collaboration)
- **No AI analysis** (just raw data)
- **Steep learning curve** (intimidating for beginners)
- **No automated reports** (manual exports)

### Our Advantage
- **Cloud-based** (access anywhere)
- **Automated PDF reports** (ready to send to clients)
- **AI interprets data** (explains WHY something is an issue)
- **No setup** (works immediately)

---

## 🔍 Deep Dive: Ahrefs Site Audit

### Strengths
- Backlink integration (domain authority context)
- Keyword rankings (see what you rank for)
- Competitor research (built-in)
- Fast crawling (1000s of pages)

### Weaknesses
- **Expensive** ($99/mo minimum, real users pay $179+)
- **Overkill** for small projects (too many features)
- **No AI recommendations**
- **No local SEO focus** (generic global)

### Our Advantage
- **Local SEO auto-detection** (Polish business schema.org)
- **Polish market focus** (we understand local context)
- **AI content analysis** (readability for Polish text)
- **Budget-friendly** (1/2 the price)

---

## 🔍 Deep Dive: GTmetrix

### Strengths
- Free tier (popular)
- Performance-focused (Lighthouse + custom metrics)
- Historical tracking
- Simple reports

### Weaknesses
- **No SEO analysis** (only performance)
- **No content analysis**
- **No competitor comparison**
- **No AI** (basic recommendations)

### Our Advantage
- **Holistic audits** (SEO + Performance + Content in one)
- **AI recommendations** (not just "compress images", but which ones + how)
- **Competitor benchmarking** (built-in)
