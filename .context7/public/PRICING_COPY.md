## Pricing FAQ

**Q: Can I change plans at any time?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan through the Billing settings.

**Q: What happens if I hit my audit limit?**
A: You will be notified and given the option to upgrade to a higher tier.

**Q: Do you offer a trial?**
A: Our Free plan is a "forever free" tier that allows you to test all core features.
