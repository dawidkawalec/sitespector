# SiteSpector - General FAQ

## Product FAQ

**Q: What tools does SiteSpector use for audits?**
A: We use Screaming Frog for SEO crawling, Google Lighthouse for performance, and Google Gemini for AI analysis.

**Q: How long does an audit take?**
A: Most audits are completed within 1-3 minutes, depending on the site size and number of competitors.

**Q: Can I share my audits with others?**
A: Yes, you can invite team members to your workspace or download professional PDF reports to share with clients.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and isolate data per workspace. We are GDPR compliant.
